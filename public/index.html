Info

On Thursday 10th April, just after the inaugural CSSConf.au, we are hosting, in another one of Australia's historical venues, a day filled with the best, and brightest minds that the JavaScript community provides, sharing their ideas, excitement and projects with you.

Melbourne Arts House, 1874

Just like last year (well technically, 2 years but who is counting?), we plan to give the JavaScript community an event to remember. A place for you to have your mind expanded and to share that with old and new friends. We will provide the food, the event, and the social activities. You just bring your enthusiasm!

As you already know, we are back to back with CSSConf.au. So if you want to make it a couple of days of non stop web action then be sure to pick up a ticket. We will be hosting a shared social event to close down, but also kick off CSSConf and JSConf respectively. Following JSConf will be another event where you will be able to share the joy with other attendees, speakers and the organisers. We are sure the day after all this excitement you will need to Decompress , and you are in luck! It is included in your ticket.

If you have any questions, please feel free to shoot the team an email

Code of Conduct

JSConf.au Australia strives to be an inclusive, safe, accessible and fun event for everyone involved. It's an important condition of participation that all organisers, sponsors and attendees follow the Conference Code of Conduct at the conference and related events.
