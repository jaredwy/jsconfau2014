Info

On Thursday 10th April, just after the inugural CSSConf.au, we are hosting, in another one of Australia's historical venues, a day filled with the best, and brightest minds that the JavaScript community provides, sharing their ideas, excitment and projects with you.

Melbourne Arts House, 1874

Just like last year (well technically, 2 years but who is counting?), we plan to give the JavaScript community an event to remember. A place for you to have your mind expanded and to share that with old and new friends. We will provide the food, the event, and the social activitis. You just bring your enthuisasm!

Code of Conduct

JSConf.au Australia strives to be an inclusive, safe, accessible and fun event for everyone involved. It's an important condition of participation that all organisers, sponsors and attendees follow the Conference Code of Conduct at the conference and related events.
