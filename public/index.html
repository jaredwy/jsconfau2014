Info

On Thursday 10th April doors will open at 8am with the event kicking off at 9am - sharp. The event is held in the North Melbourne Meat Market and is easily accesible with the 55 and 59 trams(visible. The Flemington Rd/Peel St and The Royal Melbourne Hospital/Grattan St stops are a block away. It is also a short cab ride from the CBD.

Melbourne Arts House, 1874

For information on how to get to the venue please consult this map. It also includes directions to the opening and closing events.

Our opening party will be held at Mr Wows Emporium starting at 8pm on the 9th of April. This event will be a shared with the closing party for CSSConf.au, so it will be a great event to meet new friends. Food and drinks will be provided.

After the conference we hope you can join us at The mercat for some BBQ provided by Fancy Hanks! This event will kick off at 7pm. Your name badge from the conference will be required for this event.

If you have any questions, please feel free to shoot the team an email.

Code of Conduct

JSConf.au Australia strives to be an inclusive, safe, accessible and fun event for everyone involved. It's an important condition of participation that all organisers, sponsors and attendees follow the Conference Code of Conduct at the conference and related events.
